Theorems §8
8.1 Equator Gap
gcd(p,2p)=p. No prime at angle ½. Gap width = 1 ring.
8.2 Mirror Lift
gcd(M−p, M+1) = gcd(M−p, p+1)
Mirrors lifting = R(p+1) ∩ {1…p−1}. Encodes (Z/(p+1)Z)*.
8.3 Bottom Sector
In [2p+1, 3p]: exactly p−1 appearances. M mod p covers (Z/pZ)* in order. p−2 lift; unique fail M=3p−1.
8.4 Upper Path
gcd((M+1)/2, M+1) = (M+1)/2 ≥ 2
Never lifts. Permanent equatorial barrier.
8.5 Lower Path
gcd((M−1)/2, M+1) = gcd(…, 2)
Lifts iff M ≡ 3 (mod 4). At prime q: lifts iff q inert in Z[i].
